## 2. Positioning & Core Themes

> For diners in Envigado and Medellín who crave authentic Mexican flavor, El Culto Cocina is an author-cuisine restaurant that turns every visit into a warm, sensory ritual — vibrant Mexican recipes, fresh ingredients and attentive table service, in a lively yet caring atmosphere.

The name plays on *"un culto a la comida"* — a devotion to food, Mexican at its heart. Carry these themes into the site:
- **Devotion to food** — literally a *culto* to Mexican cuisine.
- **Memory & emotion** — flavors that awaken memories; *emoción sensorial en cada bocado*.
- **Authenticity & craft** — authentic recipes, slow-cooked meats, house-made salsas, *"de la casa."*
- **Care & restoration** — *restaurar* = to restore; a place that reminds you that you deserve to be cared for.
- **Festive Mexican spirit** — color, chiles, papel picado — balanced against an elegant, minimal core.

## 4. Brand Voice

**Tone in three words: Warm · Sensory · Authentic.** Celebratory yet grounded; never flashy. Spanish primary; light bilingual touch.

**Do:** lead with feeling and the senses · celebrate Mexican origin, color, craft · use the language of care and ritual · keep Spanish primary and sentences unhurried.
**Don't:** sound corporate/hype-y/discount-driven · over-explain or use jargon · go too minimal/cold and lose the festive warmth.

**Ready-to-use lines:**
- "Sabores que despiertan memorias."
- "Un culto a la comida con origen mexicano."
- "Una fiesta de sabores que deleitan todos tus sentidos."
- "Cocina de autor con sabor a México — ingredientes frescos, recetas auténticas."
- "Detén el tiempo y elige el amor propio como ingrediente principal."

| `rojo` | Rojo Chile | `#9A2B1E` | 154, 43, 30 | CTAs, highlights, prices |
| `barro` | Barro (Terracotta) | `#C2702F` | 194, 112, 47 | Secondary accent / warmth |
| `maiz` | Maíz (Gold) | `#EFC25E` | 239, 194, 94 | Highlights, dividers, hover |

Core identity = **cream + ink**; the four festive accents come from food + papel picado. Use accents with restraint.

### Typography
- **Display / headings:** elegant high-contrast serif — **Playfair Display** or **Cinzel** (wordmark feel), **Lora** as alt.
- **Body / UI:** clean friendly sans — **Poppins** (preferred) or **Montserrat**.
- Short serif headers: all-caps, generous letter-spacing (echo the logo).

### Logo & motifs
- **Logo:** minimal line-art tomato in a thin circle, "EST / 2021", serif "EL CULTO" + spaced "COCINA", on cream. Generous clear space. Dark-ink on light; reversed cream over dark/photographic. (Source: `Brand_assets/Culto_Brand.png` — rebuild as transparent PNG/SVG for web.)
- **Motifs:** line-art tomato icon · papel picado banners (in the 4 accents) · sombreros & chiles · elegant serif headers with small decorative borders · vibrant close-up food photography as the main visual energy.

### Spice-level system ("Nivel de picante")
Add as a small menu legend + per-dish icon:
- **Picante fuerte** — Strong / hot
- **Picante medio** — Medium
- **Ligeramente picante** — Mild
- **Mermelada dulce** — Sweet (house jam)

## 7. Audience & Atmosphere

Locals and food lovers across Envigado / greater Medellín seeking authentic, vibrant Mexican food in a relaxed but characterful setting. Both dine-in (table service is part of the promise) and delivery/takeout via WhatsApp.

**Atmosphere:** lively, colorful Mexican space — outdoor patio energy, street-art murals, papel picado, plants, warm string lighting. The site should pair that festive, photogenic warmth with the elegant, minimal logo and cream palette so it feels **crafted, not kitschy**.

## 8. Website Recommendations

**Structure (Spanish-first, optional English):**
1. **Home / Hero** — logo on cream or over a vibrant food photo; tagline; CTAs *Ver menú* · *Pedir por WhatsApp*.
2. **Menú** — full menu by section (tables above); spice legend; COP prices; flipbook link as fallback.
3. **Nosotros / Filosofía** — brand story + manifesto (ES/EN), chef-signed.
4. **Galería** — food + venue photography.
5. **Visítanos / Contacto** — address + Google Map, hours, WhatsApp button, Instagram.
6. **Pedir / Domicilios** — WhatsApp ordering CTA + any delivery-app links.

**Conversion & UX:**
- "Pedir por WhatsApp" = persistent, high-contrast button (Rojo Chile or Verde Culto).
- Show hours + location prominently (first thing people ask).
- Lead with strong food photography; keep chrome minimal on cream.
- **Mobile-first** — most traffic is the Instagram bio link.

**SEO starting points (ES):** restaurante mexicano Envigado · comida mexicana Envigado / Medellín · tacos en Envigado · cocina de autor mexicana Medellín · domicilios comida mexicana Envigado.
